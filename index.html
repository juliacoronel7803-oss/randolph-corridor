Community-Led Restoration

Greening the heart of Southeast LA.

Transforming the underutilized Randolph Street railroad into a 6-mile urban sanctuary connecting Huntington Park, Bell, Maywood, Vernon, and Commerce.

Learn More Follow Progress

Our Commitment

A Mission for Health and Equity

"Creating a future where every South LA resident breathes clean air, enjoys flourishing green spaces, and has equitable access to mobility and opportunity."

We imagine a community defined by health, cultural pride, and shared public spaces that strengthen connection and well-being. By addressing historical neglect, we are building a corridor that serves as both an environmental filter and a social bridge.

Air Quality

Reducing PM2.5 and industrial pollutants through native tree canopies.

Heat Mitigation

Combating the urban heat island effect in underserved Latinx communities.

6-Mile Greenway

Connecting five major cities with safe, protected active transportation.

The Impact

Education & Advocacy

Health Literacy

Educating our community on the risks of particulate matter and nitrogen oxides common in our industrial neighborhood.

Overlooked Advocacy

Advocating for communities often excluded from major developments, ensuring residents' voices lead the design.

Economic Benefits

Uplifting Southeast LA by improving land value and creating local maintenance jobs within the green corridor.
