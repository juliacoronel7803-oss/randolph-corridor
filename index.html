Current Reality

Addressing Environmental Neglect

Urban Heat Islands

South LA's lack of tree canopy results in extreme heat zones that disproportionately affect elderly and youth residents.

Action: 2,000+ Native Trees →

Air Quality Crisis

High rates of respiratory illness due to heavy industrial corridors and lack of natural filtration barriers.

Action: Air Quality Monitoring →

Physical Barriers

The current railroad is a magnet for dumping and a barrier that separates neighborhoods and prevents safe walking.

Action: Reconnecting Streets →

Project Benefits

Economic & Community Empowerment

Beyond the environment, Julia's research highlights how this project stimulates the local economy through job creation and property value stabilization.

  • Local Workforce: Prioritizing hiring from Southeast LA for construction and long-term garden maintenance.
  • Sustainable Upkeep: Nima's maintenance plan ensures native plant gardens thrive with minimal water and cost.
